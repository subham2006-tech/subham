Movies, also known as films, are a form of visual storytelling that combines moving images, sound, and often music to create narratives or convey artistic expression. They are a major component of the entertainment industry and can vary widely in genre, style, and format. Movies are typically categorized into genres such as drama, comedy, action, horror, science fiction, romance, thriller, and fantasy. They can be feature-length, typically running from 90 to 180 minutes, or short films, which are shorter in duration. Movies can be animated or live-action and can be produced for theatrical release, television, or streaming platforms.
