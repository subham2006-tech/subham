WELCOME TO OUR WEBSITE

Mickey Mouse is an iconic cartoon character created by Walt Disney and Ub Iwerks. He made his first appearance in the short film "Steamboat Willie" on November 18, 1928. Since then, Mickey has become the mascot of The Walt Disney Company and a cultural icon worldwide. Mickey is characterized by his cheerful personality, distinctive voice, and signature red shorts, large yellow shoes, and white gloves. Over the decades, he has starred in numerous animated films, TV shows, and comic strips, and he has appeared in various forms of media, including theme parks, merchandise, and video games.






Minnie Mouse is a beloved cartoon character created by Walt Disney and Ub Iwerks. She first appeared alongside Mickey Mouse in "Steamboat Willie" in 1928. Minnie is known for her sweet personality, her iconic polka-dotted dress, bow, and her love for music and dancing. Minnie is often depicted as Mickey Mouse's girlfriend and shares many adventures with him. Over the years, she has become a prominent character in her own right, starring in numerous animated films, TV shows, and comic strips. She also appears in Disney theme parks, merchandise, and various other media.
